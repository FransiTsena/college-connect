About

I am Atlabachew.

Welcome to my portfolio site, where I am excited to share my journey, skills and researches. I am always interested in the field of education and its potential to transform lives. I have been teaching for the last 22 years at Adama, Rift Valley, Saint Mary’s and Arsi universities, and published four English supplementary materials, two monographs and a poem. I also wrote and published 26 research articles which focused on teaching-learning, distance education, quality education, technology assisted learning and the environment. I gave training on active learning methods, classroom testing, lesson plan preparation, cheating, reading strategies, proposal writing, journal article writing, research methods, English for Secretary and Hotel English.

Objective

Dynamic Consultancy and Training PLC is set up by PhD holders who have been teaching and researching for more than 20 years. It is established to fill the gaps in the area of quality of education at primary, secondary and tertiary levels by offering training to students and teachers on learning and teaching, reading strategies, methodologies, testing, research, producing supplementary materials, supervising English teachers, and assessing quality of services of the teaching learning process.
